Why Choose Us?

At Landscape Solutions and Design Inc. we have a passion for what we do, and we strive to exceed our customer’s expectations.  We work with our customers on a personal level to build relationships and give the best possible experience with our company.  We set ourselves apart from the average landscaping companies because of our ability to combine vision with years of experience and knowledge and always work with a perfectionist mindset.

Customer service is our number one priority and it’s a team effort here at Landscape Solutions & Design Inc.  You can depend on us for high quality and service when it comes to your outdoor needs.   Connect with us today to schedule a free consultation.

Here’s How We Do Business

Initial Consultation

We provide a free consultation to review your project first-hand and discuss what your vision is for your property.  Don’t know what you want?  No problem – we can suggest the best options for your landscape based on your needs and budget. Our goal is to provide a solution you will love.

If you have inspiration photos of what you’re looking for, that’s great.  We can also show you examples of similar projects we have done to help you visualize the options.

Project Estimate

After your consultation, we will prepare a detailed estimate for your project.  This will cover all of the items we will be providing and the labor to install your new landscape or hardscape. We provide a very detailed quote for each customer and take the time to answer all questions about the project.

When you accept our estimate and provide the deposit, we schedule it and provide you with an estimated date of completion.

Project Installation

We provide a dedicated team of our experienced technicians (not outsourced labor) to install your project. The project manager will be your primary contact and keep you up to speed with the progress and any items that require your decision, related to the design implementation of your vision.

From the start of Installation through your project’s final punch list, we are there with you. Our goal is always to ensure delivery of your vision.
